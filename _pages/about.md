I'm a PhD student at [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/) and the [MRC Laboratory of Medical Sciences](https://lms.mrc.ac.uk/). My project lies at the intersection of applied mathematics and developmental biology, and I'm co-supervised by Dr. Omer Karin (head of the [Karin Lab](https://karin-lab.github.io/website/), Imperial College London) and Dr. Vicki Metzis (head of the [Metzis Lab](https://metzislab.org/), MRC LMS).

My current research focuses on the mathematical modelling of biological systems, specifically gene regulatory networks involved in the cellular differentiation processes that happen during embryonic development. A key goal of my work is to integrate biomathematical approaches with the increasing amount of available bioinformatic data in order to understand the transcriptional mechanisms that underlie cell fate decisions during development.

When it comes to being outside of the office, I enjoy engaging in all kinds of sports, playing the guitar while mumbling lyrics, going to the cinema and checking out the local scene.
